About Lisa

Biography

Lalisa Manobal, known mononymously as Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer, dancer, and actress. Born March 27, 1997, she is widely regarded as one of the most influential K-pop idols globally. She is the main dancer, lead rapper, sub-vocalist, and maknae of BLACKPINK.

She trained for 5 years under YG Entertainment after winning a 2010 audition in Thailand. BLACKPINK debuted in 2016 with “Boombayah” and “Whistle,” and has since become one of the biggest girl groups in history.

Lisa debuted solo in 2021 with “Lalisa” and “Money,” breaking global records. She later founded LLOUD and released her first full album “Alter Ego” in 2025, featuring collaborations with Doja Cat, Rosalía, Future, and more.

She is also a global fashion icon, ambassador for Celine and Bulgari, and has appeared in major acting roles including The White Lotus Season 3.
